Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how to request deletion of your data from {{APP_NAME}}, including data received from Meta (Facebook/Instagram).

Option 1 — In the app

  1. Log in at {{FRONTEND_URL}}
  2. Go to Settings → request account deletion (or contact your admin)
  3. Disconnect social accounts under Publisher Connect

Option 2 — Email request

POST to /api/v1/legal/data-deletion-request with JSON { "email": "your@email.com" }. You will receive a confirmation code to track status at /api/v1/legal/deletion-status?code=….

Meta (Facebook / Instagram) users

If you connected via Facebook Login, you can also remove the app in Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites. Our Meta Data Deletion Callback URL is registered with Meta and will revoke tokens and disconnect your social accounts.

What we delete

  • Account profile and refresh tokens
  • Connected social account tokens
  • Content and media may be retained in backups for up to 30 days, then purged

Status check

After Meta initiates deletion, visit {{FRONTEND_URL}}/data-deletion with your confirmation code.
